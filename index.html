About Me

Your Trusted Freelance WordPress Developer.

With over 13 years of professional experience, I am Umer Zafar, a seasoned software engineer who graduated with a BS in Software Engineering from Punjab University, Lahore in October 2013. My career began at a multinational software company, where I worked as a full-stack developer across various technologies, honing my technical skills and industry insights. In 2014, I ventured into WordPress development, expanding my expertise by working on freelance platforms such as Fiverr and Upwork. Over time, I transitioned to handling agency clients, allowing me to deepen my understanding of WordPress development, from theme customization to building bespoke themes, plugins, and multi-site solutions.

I have in-depth expertise in PHP, .NET, jQuery, CSS3, JavaScript, and related technologies, which further enhance my ability to deliver robust and scalable solutions. By 2017, I began to broaden my skill set, diving into digital marketing, content marketing, SEO, and eCommerce. This diverse knowledge empowers me to deliver comprehensive WordPress development services with a marketing and SEO perspective, ensuring that my clients receive not just functional websites but also strategically positioned digital platforms. Currently, I am a full-time freelance WordPress developer, catering to the needs of small agencies and large organizations alike. My work goes beyond client projects; I am also dedicated to my personal ventures, creating SaaS applications, tools-based projects, and niche blogs, all driven by my own content marketing strategies. When I am not immersed in development or marketing, you can find me exploring the great outdoors, indulging in my love for traveling, mountaineering, and trekking. Let’s collaborate and bring your WordPress vision to life with a seamless blend of development expertise and strategic marketing insight.
